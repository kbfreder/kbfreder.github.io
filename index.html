About me

My name is Kendra Frederick. I am an aspiring Data Scientist with a background in Food Quality, Biopharmaceuticals, and protein biochemistry. I am proficient in Python and know my way around a SQL query. I love exploring, visualizing, and modeling data. While I love losing myself in the numbers, I also can consider the context: does the data, or our model, tell us something new about our world? I also enjoy anagrams.
